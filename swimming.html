Benefits of Swimmimg

Swimming is a great workout because you need to move your whole body against the resistance of the water. Swimming is a good all-round activity because:

  1. Swimming is a good all-round activity because.
  2. Keeps your heart rate up but takes some of the impact stress off your body.
  3. Builds endurance, muscle strength and cardiovascular fitness.
  4. Helps you maintain a healthy weight, healthy heart and lungs.
  5. Tones muscles and builds strength.
  6. Provides an all-over body workout, as nearly all of your muscles are used during swimming.

Conditions of Swimmimg

Swimming conditioning workouts enable swimmers to improve their musculature out of the water, while maintaining a regular swim schedule:

  1. Always swim with a buddy.
  2. Swim only in areas that have a lifeguard.
  3. Stay out of the water when you are very tired, very cold, or overheated.
  4. Follow all swimming rules posted at the swimming area
  5. Obey the lifeguard's instruction.
