Group Excercise

Group activity may not be a new concept but it has certainly seen massive international up-trends over the last twenty years with rapidly rising numbers in spin cycling, aerobic and dance-based classes and the emergence of CrossFit and its tribe mentality. working out in a motivational pack or using it as a tool to enhance internal or external competitive performance is fast becoming the preferred form of exercise.Not only is group fitness having a moment, but it's having a significant impact on our health, too.

Benefits of Excercise

It is performed for various reasons, to aid growth and improve strength:

  1. Meet like minded people and make friends. Training with a mate is great for motivation.Training with others creates a fun atmosphere – as they say, time flies when you are having fun!
  2. No matter what your style is, there is a class for you. Pilates, Yoga, Rev, Bootcamp, Step or Zumba – everything you desire is under the one roof! Check out the timetable.
  3. All of our instructors are qualified and experienced – you’ll get the safest, most effective workout every time.
  4. If you haven’t trained for a while or are new to the gym you’ll have someone making sure you know what to do. Take the guess work out of your workout.
  5. All of our classes are”freestyle.” No pre-choreography means variety you can’t get anywhere else. No boredom!.
