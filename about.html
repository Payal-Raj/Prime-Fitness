REASON TO TRY EXERCISE

Exercise gives us energy

Regular physical activity makes your muscles, heart, and lungs stronger, giving you more energy and strength to tackle everyday chores like shoveling snow and hauling in groceries.

Exercise helps us sleep better

Exercise can help to give you a break from your worries.6 While you’re exercising, your focus is on what you’re doing. This could be your swimming stroke, your running pace, what your teammates are doing

Exercise controls weight

Exercising is an excellent way to lose weight and to help keep it off. When we exercise we burn calories, with more intense exercises burning more calories.
